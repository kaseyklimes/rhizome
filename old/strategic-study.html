STRATEGIC STUDY
5 WEEKS | MIXED METHOD | INCLUDES WORKSHOPS

Asymmetric product strategy begins with deep user insight.

The Strategic Study is an expedition into a key market or user segment, deploying an interdisciplinary suite of qualitative and quantitative tools to rigorously chart high-value opportunities for product-market fit and major risks to be circumvented. You'll join us as we guide you into the world and mind of your user, uncovering foundational mental models and identifying unmet needs.

EXAMPLE
In 2021, a venture studio came to Rhizome R&D with interest in market opportunities at the point of transaction for brick and mortar retail. Our Strategic Study led us behind the scenes of New York City's busiest restaurants, time-honored hotels, and most beloved small businesses. We followed business owners and managers as they revealed the challenges they face in a competitive environment. When the space constraints of Manhattan commercial real estate prevented our client team from shadowing, we provided a private live-stream of the journey from our GoPro cameras.
